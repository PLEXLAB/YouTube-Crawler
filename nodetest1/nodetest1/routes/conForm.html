WHAT IS THE PURPOSE AND PROCEDURE OF THIS STUDY?

The purpose of this study is to conduct a distributed investigation of the YouTube video platform's demonetization policies using an automated browser extension tool. This information has the potential to inform understanding of the practices of content creators and the impacts of platform policy updates. This understanding may inform the design of future systems.

If you choose to participate in this study, you will be asked to install a chrome extension built by the research team. The extension will access your YouTube channel creator studio page and crawl the meta-data and the analytics of the videos you have published on your channel. When crawling, the extension will open a minimized window in your browser, which automatically navigate through pages of the YouTube Studio pages for your channel. Collected data will be stored in a secure server maintained by the research team, physically located at NMSU. Collected data will be destroyed 5 years after the completion of the study.

WHY ARE WE ASKING YOU TO PARTICIPATE FOR THIS STUDY?

You are being asked to participate in this study because you are content creator on one or more social media platforms.

ARE THERE REASONS WHY YOU WOULD NOT QUALIFY FOR THIS STUDY?

WHAT IS THE DURATION?

The duration of the process crawling your YouTube channel will be based on the number of videos you have posted with approximation of 10 minutes. The crawling process will happen periodically, every day, until the completion of the study. The study is scheduled to conclude January 31, 2021. You may choose to uninstall the extension before that to stop the crawling process.

CONFIDENTIALITY

The Researcher(s) will keep your name and other identifying information private to the extent that we can.

If you consent to the use of your data in future studies below, your participation and metadata (i.e. video monetization, recommendation, and advertisement analytics data) may be shown to future participants.

Otherwise, the Researcher(s) will make every effort to prevent anyone who is not on the research team from knowing that you gave us information, or what that information is. You should know that there are some circumstances in which the Researcher(s) may have to show your information to other people.

Information collected in the process of this study will remain confidential. Researchers will follow the laws and regulations regarding privacy practices in this domain. We will keep a copy of your contact information separate from study data in order to contact you for follow ups if needed. This will be destroyed along with all data 5 years after the completion of the study.

The findings and results of the study may appear publicly in journals, conferences, and other academic publications.

Your anonymized data may be shown to other users of the extension to help them understand the impact of platform policies at a later time.
